# Google Gemma 4 - Open Models for local and edge intelligence

> **Headquarters:** Mountain View, California, USA | **Founded:** 2024 (Gemma Family) | **Stage:** Open Weights Ecosystem | **Website:** [ai.google.dev/gemma](https://ai.google.dev/gemma)

---

## One-Line Summary

Google Gemma is a family of lightweight, state-of-the-art open models built from the same research and technology used to create the Gemini models, designed for local deployment, optimization, and edge application.

---

## Why Google Gemma 4?

Most proprietary AI models require continuous cloud connectivity, massive server infrastructure, and complex API pricing. Google's Gemma models (specifically the latest releases such as Gemma 2 2B, 9B, and 27B, alongside specialised models like PaliGemma and CodeGemma) democratize access to state-of-the-art intelligence. 

By delivering high performance at compact model sizes, Gemma enables developers and builders to compile, quantize, and run advanced language and vision-language capabilities directly on local hardware - including consumer-grade laptops, edge servers, and single-GPU embedded systems. This makes Gemma the ideal open-weights framework for offline-first, private, and localized intelligence in regions with poor connectivity.
